****# SQL-Project

The given SQL queries are used to retrieve specific information from a database.

The first set of queries aims to find the senior most employee based on job title, countries with the most invoices, top 3 values of total invoice, the city with the best customers, and the best customer based on the highest spending.

The second set of queries involves retrieving details about rock music listeners, top rock bands, and tracks with a song length longer than the average song length.

The advance queries focus on the total amount spent by each customer on artists, and the most popular music genre for each country.

These queries are designed to extract precise data from the database and provide insights into various aspects such as employee hierarchy, customer spending, music genre preferences, and track details. The results can be used for decision-making, analysis, and strategic planning.****
